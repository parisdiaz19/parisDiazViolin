I am Paris Diaz

Professional Violinist/ Music Educator

Biography

Paris Diaz is a professional violinist that specializes in string instruments. He is a passionate music educator and mentor that constantly pushes his students to the best of their abilities.

Paris graduated from Montclair State University and holds a Bachelor's degress in Music and is a certified New Jersey Music Educator. He has performed in various venues such as Carnegie Hall, John J. Cali Music School, and Towson University.

Paris has experience teaching students of all ages. He has also worked in school districts across New Jersey such as Leonia, Ridgewood, and Fort Lee.
